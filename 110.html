February 3, 2016

Save Our Schools! Save Our World Language Programs!

by Fairfax FLAGS

Elected officials are making funding decisions right now. We need you to show your support for full funding for FCPS and the preservation of our world language programs!

  1. FLAGS Action Meeting: Please join us for an open meeting on Thursday, Feb. 11, at 6:30 PM to learn more about the FCPS budget crisis and what you can do to save our schools and world language programs.

    All FCPS schools, parents, and community members are invited to participate. The meeting will be held at Orange Hunt Elementary School - Cafeteria, 6820 Sydenstricker Rd, Springfield.

  2. Invest in Kids Rally: Please join us to advocate for full funding for our schools during an indoor rally on Tuesday, Feb. 16, from 4:45-5:45 pm at the indoor amphitheater of the Fairfax County Government Center (12000 Government Center Pkwy, Fairfax).

    We need ALL parents, students, and community members to come out and make a strong and visible show of support, so the Board of Supervisors, which contributes 70% of the FCPS budget, provides the necessary funding for FCPS.

    The rally is co-sponsored by Fairfax FLAGS, #IamFCPS, Save Fairfax Band and Strings (FACE), Fairfax Education Association, and Fairfax County Council of PTAs.

  3. Contact Your Elected Officials! Click here for form letters and email addresses for the School Board, the Board of Supervisors, and State Legislators. In less than 5 minutes, you can influence your elected officials to fully fund our schools and preserve our world language programs.

    Only a few people have sent emails so far, and that is not enough to move the needle. Please send an email today, so your elected officials hear your voice!

    If you previously sent an email, please send another one today using the form letters. Every letter counts!
