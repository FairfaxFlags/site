February 10, 2016

FLAGS - Prepare to Rally!

by Fairfax FLAGS

I would like to request your support to sustain the quality of our public schools, which affects our kids, our property values, and the local economy.

  1. Please attend our FCPS Budget Advocacy Action Meeting on Thursday, Feb. 11, at 6:30 PM at Orange Hunt ES -- Cafeteria, 6820 Sydenstricker Rd, Springfield. This meeting is co-sponsored by #IamFCPS.
    • All are welcome, including kids!
    • Learn more about the FCPS budget and what they can do to ensure our schools are fully funded.
    • All participants will have the opportunity to ask questions, share their knowledge and experience, propose new ideas, and make connections with participants from other schools across the county.
    • During the meeting, adults and kids are also invited to participate in the making of signs for our Invest in Kids rally/event on Tues., Feb. 16. A limited supply of poster boards and art supplies will be available, but you are welcome to bring additional paper/poster/art supplies.
  2. Please rally to support full funding for our schools at the indoor "Invest in Kids" event on Tuesday, Feb. 16, at 4:45-6:00 PM at the Fairfax County Government Center -- Indoor Atrium, 12000 Government Center Pkwy, Fairfax.
    • Since 2008, Fairfax County Public Schools (FCPS) has had to cut nearly $500 million from its budget. We have now reached a critical juncture. There is nothing left to cut, except the core programs and services that have made our schools world class. We can begin to re-invest in our schools and maintain their reputation and tradition of excellence, or FCPS will have to undertake destructive budget cuts that will impact our kids, our property values, and the local economy.
    • We are seeking to mobilize hundreds of concerned citizens to make a strong and visible show of support to persuade the Fairfax County Board of Supervisors to provide full funding for our schools.
    • This event is the most important event during the entire budget process. The Board of Supervisors provides 70% of FCPS funding, so its funding decision will determine the future quality of our schools.
    • The event is co-sponsored by #IamFCPS, Fairfax County Council of PTAs, Fairfax Education Association, Fairfax Arts Council for Education, and Fairfax FLAGS.
  3. Please share this information with anyone who wants to preserve the quality of Fairfax County Public Schools.
V/r,
Matt Dunne
Fairfax FLAGS
