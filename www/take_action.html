Take Action

Focus on advocacy that will change the votes of elected officials:

  • Speak at a public hearing
  • Meet with your elected officials in person
  • Write a personal message, and send by email, fax, mail, or other means
  • Call your elected officials

Avoid advocacy that will not change their votes:

  • Social media (Facebook, Twitter, etc.)
  • Form letters (unless you add a personal message)

  • Contact elected officials for your base school and immersion school
  • Contact at-large officials (they represent everyone!)

Speak at a Public Hearing

  • We need a diverse array of speakers, including parents and students, to support of full funding and saving FCPS language immersion programs.
  • Board of Supervisors budget hearing -- April 5, 6, or 7, 2016. Fairfax County Government Center, 12000 Government Center Pkwy, Fairfax. Register now here (request an evening speaking slot). Ask them for 4-cent tax increase and full funding for FCPS.
  • School Board budget hearing -- May 17, 2016. Luther Jackson MS, 3020 Gallows Rd, Falls Church. Register here beginning on May 6 (request evening speaking slot). Ask them to save FCPS language immersion programs.

Meet with Elected Officials

  • Arrange a meeting at their office or another location. Bring as many people as possible!
  • Meet them at town hall meetings or other events. See “Calendar” link above.

Call Elected Officials

  • Schedule a time to speak with your elected officials.
  • Give your message to their assistant.
  • Call more than once! The more calls, the greater the impact!

Write to Elected Officials

  • Send a personal message by email.
  • Write a short note and send by fax or mail.
  • Organize a letter writing party at your home or school.
  • Write more than once! The more letters, the greater the impact!
